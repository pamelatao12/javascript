Hello, my name is Pamela Tao.

Welcome to my website! I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area in California, and am currently based out of New York City. I graduated Cornell University in the Fall of 2017 with a degree in Business.

From strategy consulting to product management, I work with Fortune 500 companies to deploy and integrate digital technologies, as well as manage consumer-facing product launches that touch millions of users a day.

From there, I found myself spending multiple hours a day diving head-first into data structures, algorithms, and personal projects. So welcome, to the entry point of my journey! This site is 100% original - and please check out my projects page for more!
